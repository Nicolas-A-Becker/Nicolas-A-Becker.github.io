Steps to Making the Cookie

  • chocolate chips
  • butter
  • flour
  • sugar
  • brown sugar
  • eggs
  • salt
  • baking powder
  • vanilla extract
  1. Mix butter and sugars together in batter
  2. beat eggs
  3. add eggs and vanilla to batter
  4. dissolve baking soda in hot water
  5. add baking soda and water to batter with salt
  6. use a spoon to place dots of dough onto baking sheet
  7. bake cookies in oven for a period of time until ready
